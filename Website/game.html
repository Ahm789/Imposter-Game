The Odd One Out Game Rules

  • Each player takes a turn to see their role or word.
  • Imposters get a hint about the word, normal players see the actual word.
  • The game continues until all players have seen their roles.
  • Imposters try to guess the word or mislead others in discussion.
  • You can adjust hints, difficulty, and number of imposters in the settings.
