The Odd One Out Game Rules

  • Each round has one secret word.
  • Players take turns privately viewing their role.
  • Most players see the secret word. Imposters receive only a hint instead.
  • After everyone has seen their role, discussion begins.
  • Describe the word without saying it directly.
  • Imposters must blend in and try to figure out the word.
  • After discussion, everyone votes for who they think the imposter is.
  • Adjust hints, difficulty, and number of imposters in the settings before starting.
